    About This Application

    VOID HEIST is a tactical sci-fi board game of betrayal, upgrades, and high-risk escapes. Set in the year 2149, players take on the roles of elite infiltrators attempting to steal Voidmatter—a rare energy source—from Helix Dominion's space station. The station AI is hunting you, security drones are adapting, and one player might secretly be working against the team.

    The game features a modular hex-based board that changes every session, with rooms like Reactor Core, Data Vault, Drone Bay, and Escape Hangar. Tiles can flip to reveal hazards, and some rooms degrade over time as the ship falls apart. Your objective: collect 3 Voidmatter cores and escape before the AI reaches Alert Level 5, the Reactor melts down, or you eliminate each other.

    Choose from 6 unique specialists—The Cypher (hacker), The Vanguard (tank), The Ghost (stealth), The Engineer (gadgets), The Analyst (prediction), and The Synthetic (mysterious power). Each has distinct abilities that shape your strategy and approach to the heist.

    KEY CAPABILITIES

    VOID HEIST features an Adaptive AI System that reacts to player actions—loud players trigger drone swarms, hackers increase firewall difficulty, and loot hoarders face "accidents." The AI evolves mid-game, learning from your strategies.

    The Betrayal Protocol adds a hidden role element: one player may secretly draw the "Corporate Asset" card, changing their mission to secure 2 cores and prevent others from escaping. The Upgrade Economy lets you spend Voidmatter to install cybernetic boosts, enhance weapons, override rooms, or sabotage others. Every decision is a trade-off between escaping rich or escaping alive.
